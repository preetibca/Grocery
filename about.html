About FreshGo

At FreshGo, we believe grocery shopping should be fresh, fast, and friendly. Founded in 2025, our mission is to deliver healthy and organic groceries to your doorstep at the best prices.

Our team works directly with local farms and trusted vendors to bring you the highest quality produce, grains, and daily essentials.

We started as a small team of 3 and have now grown into a full-service online grocery store with thousands of happy customers.

Our motto is simple: Eat Fresh, Live Better!

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and affordable online grocery store in India.

Our Mission

To make quality food accessible to every household with ease and affordability.
